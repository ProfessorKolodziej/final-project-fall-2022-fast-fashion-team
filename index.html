About

Welcome to our website on sustianable fashion and eco-friendly clothing. In the last couple of years, fast-fashion has been on the rise where many people are buying clothes made from cheap material and produced by child labor. Most of these online retailers offer clothing at a an affordabe price that comes with a cost: they are not long lasting and are not sustaniable. Here we want to educate you on how to stop buying fast-fashion and find alternative ways to buy clothing this is safe for the environment. You will learn about different fabrics, sustainable ways to shop, resources to use and how you can personally make a difference.
